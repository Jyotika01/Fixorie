Our Team

FixOrie

FixOrie is all about finding a plumber, beautician, electrician, etc. For the majority of people, it is hard to find trusted and trained professionals for their in-house work. Therefore, FixOrie is a medium, where the users can find professionals for all of their in-house work, and the professionals can find work.
